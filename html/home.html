About Us

Circus Restaurant is a unique restaurant and cocktail bar in Central Stockholm open since 2020. The restaurant located in the perfect place in Stockholm for lunch, afterwork drinks, before dinner cocktails, dinner, perfect entertainment and a late-night bar. The circus shows open only Friday and Saturday between 15:00 to 23:00. We have popular Indian menu with meat and vegetarian food options.
